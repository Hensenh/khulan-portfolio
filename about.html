About Me

I am a UX designer based in Los Angeles, CA . Due to my curious nature, I would like to say that I am a Jane-of-most trades. I have a background in Business, Marketing, Psychology and a dash of front-end coding along with some knowledge in back-end. I recently graduated from a UX bootcamp at General Assembly to help hone my skills as a UX Designer.

My passion for UX design stems from a need to craft meaningful experiences for people through creating functional and usable solutions. My focus on UX is taking on a person-centric approach. By empathizing with people, I can understand what their needs are and what issues they may currently face. By talking to people, I can create those meaningful experiences. The best part about UX is being able to help people people improve their day-to-day lives through meaningful designs (no matter how small or large the contribution is).

Currently, I am freelancing with In Creative Company to help redesign their website. I am also on the look out for new UX opportunities, projects, and jobs within the Los Angeles and Santa Monica area. Feel free to reach out to me -- I would be glad to chat about work or play!
